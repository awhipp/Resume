Investment Portfolio

  • November 15, 2021
  • By Alexander Whipp

As a long term retail investor, this page showcases a live snapshot of my current investments and trading methodology.

My trading style is primarily focused on:

  • Long term sustained and conservative growth in stocks across sectors
  • Focus on stocks and ETFs with historically low volatility and regular dividend yield
  • Where possible the equities are evenly distributed within my portfolio. If either yeild is cut or percentage change falls below a designated threshold then a stock is just held and not invested until those numbers recover.
  • Regular deposits and dividend reinvestment plans (DRIPs) ensure that I can rebalance my portfolio accordingly to balance any market movements
