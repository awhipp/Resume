Career in a Nutshell

Goal: To be technologically savvy as well as competent in business to serve as a valuable asset between the desires of management and a development team's capabilities. My professional and academic career are evidence of my multifaceted interests and skill-sets.
Since 2018, I have worked as an Associate at Booz Allen Hamilton serving the Health analytics market.
From 2016-2018, I have worked at Zoomdata to help clients solve business intelligence problems by integrating their existing technology stacks into the multifaceted visualization suite.
From 2015-2016, I was the customer success lead and a developer on the Sailfish product suite--a strategic investment account within the NextGen Analytics at Booz Allen Hamilton.
In 2011, I attended the University of Maryland where I earned three degrees in my four year tenure for computer science, psychology, and business analytics.
